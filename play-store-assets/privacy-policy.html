Privacy Policy for Code Compass

Last updated: August 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Code Compass is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about our users. The app operates primarily using local device storage.

2. Local Data Storage

Code Compass uses your device's local storage to:

  • Remember your app preferences and settings
  • Cache CSA code content for offline access
  • Maintain your subscription status locally
  • Store your search history on your device only

This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to LARK Labs or any third parties.

3. Subscription and Payment

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely through Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All subscription management is handled by Google Play Store according to their privacy policies.

4. No Personal Data Collection

Code Compass does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any personal or sensitive information

5. Third-Party Services

Code Compass may use Google Play Services for subscription management. These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for professional use by HVAC technicians and contractors. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Website: https://larklabs.org
